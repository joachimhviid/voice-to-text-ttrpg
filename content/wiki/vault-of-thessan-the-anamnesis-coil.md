## Chronicle

The journey into the Vault of Thessan was fraught with danger and calculated risk. Beneath the echoing footsteps of the golem patrol, the party utilized a narrow service tunnel, navigating by the light of a single, pale blue drift-globe that illuminated the antechamber. Despite initial bickering and delays with the masterwork grate lock, they managed to transition into the main passage. Rook's careful reconnaissance was crucial, revealing that the vault lay through the right-hand door, while the passage straight ahead led to the undesirable curator's office. Upon passing through the right door, the party entered a long, narrow passage lined with sealed iron canisters. At the far end was the reinforced door, clad in copper banding—a construction suggesting either powerful magical containment or extreme sensitivity. After several moments of deliberation, Caden proceeded to check for visible traps, while Mira proved exceptionally skilled, identifying a nearly invisible tripwire set six inches off the ground, attached to an old-fashioned iron bell. The final lock proved manageable despite the numbness induced by the copper banding. Beyond the door, the party found the objective: a single, silver-white coil suspended in a velvet-lined case—the Anamnesis Coil. While the initial analysis of the coil showed nothing amiss, the sudden resurgence of grinding stone sounds from the antechamber signaled immediate peril. When Mira finally lifted the Coil, the antechamber's drift-globe flared white, and the copper banding on the vault door began to hum. Realizing the secondary golem was active and closing in, Mira made the decision to retrieve the artifact, prompting the final confrontation.

## Summary

- The party infiltrated the Vault of Thessan via a service tunnel, overcoming complex locks and navigating the main antechamber.
- The Anamnesis Coil was located behind a reinforced, copper-banded door at the far end of a narrow passage.
- Mira discovered a concealed tripwire, demonstrating exceptional skill despite the magical and physical barriers.
- The attempt to retrieve the Anamnesis Coil triggered a devastating cascade of events: the antechamber's lights flared, and the vault door began to hum.
- The discovery activated a secondary golem, initiating a final, desperate rush into the vault as the party was cornered.
