## Chronicle

The infiltration of the Vault of Thessan began in the confines of a service tunnel, where the three adventurers knelt in cold, iron-smelling water. After establishing the patrol rotation pattern of the golem guard above, the group prepared to pass through a masterwork grate lock. Despite Caden's impatience, Mira worked methodically on the mechanism, eventually succeeding where force would have failed. Emerging into the antechamber, they found the space dimly lit by a single, pale blue drift-globe, revealing two doors: one straight ahead, leading to the curator's office, and another to the right, which Rook knew housed the vault. 

After an initial survey noted a slightly brighter drift-globe—indicating recent presence—the momentary cessation of the golem patrol offered a critical window. The group quickly transitioned into the right passage, though Rook’s hasty exit caused them to fall, echoing loudly in the chamber. Just as the secondary golem began its menacing, grinding approach, Caden hauled the injured Rook through the door and into a narrow, specialized passage. The passage was lined with iron canisters and culminated in a heavy door, reinforced with copper banding, signaling the presence of something highly magical or sensitive. 

Despite initial difficulty assessing the wards, Mira proceeded alone, first spotting and circumventing a nearly invisible tripwire placed near the passage's ten-foot mark. She finally manipulated the unique lock, which yielded without incident. Inside, the Anamnesis Coil sat in a simple, velvet-lined case. Rook expressed suspicion that the coil was the only item in the room, suggesting a deeper trap. When the secondary golem resumed its mechanical approach from the antechamber, Mira moved to retrieve the artifact. The moment she lifted the silver-white coil, the drift-globe flared white and the copper-banded door began to hum, confirming the activation of a protective mechanism. With the golem now visibly closing the distance, the group retreated. Rook attempted to deploy a smoke flask to slow the approaching foe, but in doing so, the bell wire was tripped, announcing their immediate location.

## Summary

- The party navigated the service tunnel beneath the Vault of Thessan, overcoming a masterwork lock under pressure.
- They advanced through the antechamber and into a specialized passage, narrowly escaping the secondary golem patrol.
- Mira located and bypassed a tripwire, accessing the final door that housed the Anamnesis Coil.
- The retrieval of the Coil triggered an immediate magical alarm, activating the door's protective wards.
- The secondary golem pursuit intensified, leading the party to a desperate and failed attempt to deploy a smoke screen.
