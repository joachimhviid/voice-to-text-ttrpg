## Chronicle

The passage into the deepest heart of the Vault of Thessan was a confluence of dripping water and foul, metallic air. After confirming the rotation cycle of the surface golem patrol, the group descended into the service tunnels, moving with practiced caution towards the central antechamber. The antechamber, illuminated by a single, pale blue drift-globe, displayed two egress points: one straight ahead into what appeared to be a curator’s office, and another to the right, which marked the suspected path to the vault’s inner sanctum. 

The initial crossing of the grate was fraught with tension, requiring Mira’s mastery of a masterwork lock. Despite Caden's impatience and Rook's timely advice regarding the vault's likely location, the group managed to slip through the passage just as the heavy footsteps above grew silent. The moment of opportunity arrived, leading them into the deeper, narrower passage. 

Here, the atmosphere shifted from tension to measured paranoia. The group noted the strange brightness of the drift-globe, evidence that someone had been in the vault recently. A significant mechanical sound—the grinding of stone on stone—emanated from the depths, signaling that something had been activated. Working together, they navigated the passages until they reached a final, heavily reinforced door, banded with copper and secured by a serious lock. This construction suggested that whatever lay beyond was either powerful magic or incredibly sensitive to external forces. 

After careful preparation, they proceeded with the retrieval. Mira expertly bypassed a nearly invisible tripwire set into the passage floor. The lock on the final door yielded easily to her efforts, revealing the room's singular occupant: the Anamnesis Coil. The artifact rested serenely in a velvet-lined case, catching light that seemed wholly artificial. 

However, the respite was brief. The humming of the copper banding and the sudden blazing white of the drift-globe signaled that the artifact had been claimed. The secondary golem, alerted by the theft, was already moving, its passage heralded by the rising crescendo of grinding stone. Cornered and needing to buy precious seconds, Rook shattered a smoke flask at the passage entrance, inadvertently triggering an alarm bell that announced their presence to the advancing threat.

## Summary

- The party infiltrated the service tunnels beneath the Vault of Thessan, navigating patrols and tension.
- They passed through the antechamber, noticing signs of recent activity and a mechanical threat source.
- The party reached the final door, reinforcing that the Anamnesis Coil was the objective.
- Mira successfully bypassed traps and opened the heavily secured door.
- The Coil was stolen, immediately triggering the secondary golem, which pursued them into a narrow passage.
